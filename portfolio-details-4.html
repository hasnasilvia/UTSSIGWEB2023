Making Brochure About The Tourism in Kalirejo Village

By creating a Kalirejo tourist village brochure, it is hoped that it can highlight local tourism in Kalirejo village, one of which is Gradhika Garden Hill or what is known as GG Hill. Apart from that, there are many photo spots and views that can be seen in Kalirejo village. This is done so that Kalirejo village tourism becomes better known to the public.
